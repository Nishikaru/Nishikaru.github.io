This is the

Trip Point Bangladesh

Bangladesh is known for its natural beauty and wonderful scenery, particularly Cox's Bazar. Located on the south-eastern side of Bangladesh, Cox's Bazar has the world's longest uninterrupted natural sea beach, stretching for 125 km. Both the sea and the hillsides along this fishing port town are extremely diverse with about 8,000 different kinds of species. The marine life includes jellyfish, starfish, several different fish species, and also two types of rare sea turtles.
